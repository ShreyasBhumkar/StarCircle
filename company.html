About StarCircle

StarCircle is a software company with a decade-long experience in digital technology, that offers a wide variety of products and solutions to the BFSI sector, with special focus on Insurance and Banking. We are amongst the first few companies with our very own digital mobility platform comprising of a server-side enterprise middleware called FREDTM(MEAP), created in 2005 and a framework, called StallionTM(MADP) which was introduced in 2011, for building OS agnostic applications. We also have the capability of harnessing the power of cognitive computing, due to our superior AI technology stack developed in-house. AccelTree offers digital products and services that cater to customer life cycle management, especially in the area of multiple interactions that take place either virtually between the customer and the organization or face-to-face through intermediaries.

We have over 25 clients including many Fortune 500 companies in the Insurance space, and 100,000+ active users. We have partnered with some of the most reputed Insurers in the world and have a rich implementation experience across geographies, having configured many complex insurance products including unit-linked products. AccelTree has also partnered with Malaysia’s major Takaful Insurance provider. By delivering innovative point of sale systems AccelTree has played a key role in the Insurance Industry.

We are proud to say that NASSCOM has not only recognized us as a Showcase Company for IT Innovation in India, but in partnership with Frost & Sullivan, it has also rated us as an ‘Exemplar’ in the product excellence matrix for enterprise mobility.
