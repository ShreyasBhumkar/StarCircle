Main features of Blockchain Technology

Blockchain is one of the emerging technologies currently in the market it gives an opportunity to radically transform the way we do things by providing a standard architecture to address different business and organisational problems.

A blockchain is a unique form of Database storage system available in the market which uses records to store data or information. Storing blocks of information that are identical across the network which get copied automatically with the mechanism of cryptography providing a more secure data storage platform that has no single point of failure & can be controlled by any single entity.
